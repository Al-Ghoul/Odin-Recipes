Waffles

Description

Waffles are a delicious breakfast or brunch food made from batter or dough cooked between patterned plates. The resulting squares or shapes have a crispy exterior and fluffy interior, perfect for soaking up syrups, fruit, and other toppings.

They come in a variety of styles around the world, but are enjoyed for their sweetness and satisfying texture.

Ingredients

  • Two Whole Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Make the batter: Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Make the waffles: Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
